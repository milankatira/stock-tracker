<objective>
Stand up the AI compliance foundation that every later phase will depend on. This plan ships the three-layer guard that makes "no AI output without compliance" enforceable by construction: (1) a class-scoped ComplianceInterceptor on AiService, (2) a private Gemini client that ai.module.ts never exports, and (3) an ESLint rule forbidding `ai.service` imports outside `jobs/` and `chat/`. It also ships the supporting compute libraries — `template-slots.ts` and `numeric-audit.ts` — which Plan 02 will use to enforce the "AI never invents a number" invariant.

Purpose: COMP-02 (single compliance chokepoint) + foundation for COMP-04 (numeric audit, template-slot). All downstream AI surfaces (narrative-batch in Plan 02, sentiment in Phase 6, chat in Phase 7) inherit this guard.

Output: A fully wired AIModule + ComplianceModule with passing fixture-based unit tests for the sanitiser, numeric audit, template-slot substitution, and interceptor behaviour. ESLint custom rule active in CI. No live Gemini calls yet — AiService methods are scaffolded with the structured-output prompt signatures from 04-RESEARCH.md Example 1, but the narrative-batch pipeline (which actually invokes them) ships in Plan 02.
</objective>
